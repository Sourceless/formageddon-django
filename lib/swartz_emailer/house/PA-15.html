Charlie Dent, United States Representative for the 15th District of Pennsylvania

Contact

E-Mail

Thank you for sending me your email. I appreciate the time you have taken to keep me informed about your concerns.

As was stated on the previous web page, if the email you just sent involves a specific time-sensitive issue and you need my staff to contact a federal agency on your behalf (VA benefit problems, immigration paperwork, postal mail being delivered incorrectly, etc.), please contact my Lehigh Valley office directly at (610) 770-3490.

I want to assure you that your email is important to me. My staff or I review every single piece of correspondence. We do this because we want to know what the people of the 15th District think about pieces of legislation (proposed or existing), the activities of the federal government, or other issues of concern to them. Although you and I may or may not agree on every issue, I will always take your views into account in deciding how to vote on or to otherwise address the issue or issues that concern you here in the Congress. Only with your input can I serve the people of the 15th District to the best of my ability.

Thank you again for taking the time to express your views to me. Your participation in the democratic process is essential, as I have no mission more important than hearing the views of my constituents as I make day-to-day decisions on legislation and other matters of importance that come before the Congress.

If you are writing to request a tour of the U.S. Capitol, White House, or other sites in Washington, DC, you do not need to fill out a privacy release form. To request a tour, please contact Colin Franey in my Washington office at 202-225-6411 or colin.franey@mail.house.gov.
With best wishes,

Sincerely,
Charlie Dent

 

If your e-mail involves a specific time-sensitive issue, and you need my staff to contact a federal agency on your behalf (VA benefit problems, immigration paperwork, passport application, postal issues, etc.), please contact my Lehigh Valley office directly at (610) 770-3490.
