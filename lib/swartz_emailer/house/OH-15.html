Write Your Rep

Thank you for contacting my office. As a Congressional courtesy, members allow one another to deal directly with their constituents in order to provide the most efficient constituent service possible.  Unfortunately, it appears that you are not a resident of Ohio’s 15th Congressional District. To find your representative, please visit http://www.house.gov and enter your zip code in the area labeled “Find Your Representative” in the top right-hand corner.

Again, thank you for contacting me.
