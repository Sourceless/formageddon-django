Contact Form

Senator Manchin is eager to hear from his constituents. To share your concerns with Senator Manchin, or ask him a question, please fill out the contact form below. Senator Manchin wants to stay in touch, so don't forget to include your phone number and/or e-mail address. 

You can call our home office in West Virginia at 304-342-5855 or Senator Manchin's Washington, D.C. office at 202-224-3954. In addition, West Virginians with a 304 or 681 area code can contact Senator Manchin's office through our toll-free number at 855-275-5737.
