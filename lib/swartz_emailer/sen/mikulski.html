Get Connected



 

Due to the increased number of e-mails to my website, I ask for your patience as my staff and I work to sort and respond to your concerns. My best ideas come from people in Maryland, and I want to address your comments and questions.

If you need help with a personal matter with a federal agency and your issue is time-sensitive, please call my Baltimore Office at (410) 962-4510. If you need help cutting through government red tape with a government program such as Social Security, Medicare, or veterans' benefits, or are having a problem with a government agency such as the Internal Revenue Service or Immigration and Naturalization Service, my staff can make an inquiry on your behalf.


Thank you for contacting Senator Mikulski's office.
