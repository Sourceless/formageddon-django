If You Need Help With a Federal Agency

It is my honor and privilege to serve the people of Wisconsin in the United States Senate. As such, I welcome the opportunity to help constituents deal with federal agencies. My professional staff is prepared to work with you to resolve issues related to: 

  • Social Security
  • Medicare and Medicaid
  • Labor and Unemployment Issues
  • Veterans’ benefits
  • Military service
  • The Internal Revenue Service
  • Immigration
  • Passports
  • Small Business Administration
  • The Environmental Protection Agency
  • Other federal matters

My professional staff are trained to help Wisconsinites with all types of constituent casework. If you require assistance, feel free to call or write one of my state offices. Alternately, feel free to submit your request for assistance through the webform below. Before my office can assist you, you will be asked to fill out and sign a copy of this privacy release form.

NOTE: if you are requesting assistance with a federal agency, to ensure proper and timely handling of this request, please be sure to choose the topic ‘casework’ from the dropdown menu. If you are contacting my office for information regarding legislation, please choose the appropriate topic area (not ‘casework’). 

NOTE: If you are a member of the press, please do not use the contact form below. You should contact Senator Johnson's Washington office at (202) 224-5323.

THANK YOU!

Thank you for sharing your opinions and concerns with me!  My office will make every effort to respond to you in a timely manner; however, due to the large volume of mail that we receive, it may take several weeks before you receive a response.
