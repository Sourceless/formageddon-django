      When elected to the U.S. Senate, Senator Ron Wyden pledged to hold at least one public town meeting in each of Oregon's 36 counties each year. Keeping that promise since 1996, Senator Wyden has held more than 655 town hall meetings across the state.  Here you will also find information about some of Oregon's most popular sites and attractions, Oregon's Congressional Delegation, and more.
