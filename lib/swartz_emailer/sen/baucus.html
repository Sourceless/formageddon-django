Contact Max

You can email Max about legislative issues from this page. Montana residents who include their full name and mailing address will receive a written response. If your email regards a request for help from Max, visit the How Can Max Help?  section. If your email regards a request for a flag or tour while you are in Washington DC, you can expedite your request by visiting the Services for Montanans section.  If you wish to contact an office by phone or mail, visit the office locations page.
