Contact Form

Dear fellow South Dakotan:

Thank you for taking the time to contact me through my website. It is good to hear from you.

This message is an automatic response sent on my behalf to let you know that I received your email message. Your thoughts and concerns are very important to me. I will review and respond to your email as soon as possible. However, if your message requires immediate attention, such as a scheduling request or casework, please call my office at 1-202-224-2321.

If you have any other thoughts or information, don't hesitate to provide further details. It would be impossible to represent the state properly without the constant input of South Dakotans. I appreciate my constituents using every means possible to contact me. Continue to keep me informed, and please let me know whenever I can be of assistance to you.

Kindest regards,


JOHN THUNE
United States Senator, South Dakota
