    Senator Carper works hard to ensure that Delawareans are kept informed of the debates and decisions that are made in our nation's Capitol, as well as the work he does for the First State every day. 

    This section offers a variety of resouces to stay up to date with Senator Carper's work in Delaware and Washington, D.C. You will find press releases, upcoming events, op-eds, pictures, videos and notable articles from the state and national media.

    Constituent Services FAQs

    This page offers answers to some of the most common questions Senator Carper's office receives from Delawareans.

    Help with Federal Agencies

    If you have an issue, problem or need guidance with a federal government agency, Senator Carper and his staff are ready to assist you.

    Assistance with Federal Grants

    Discover resources to help eligible grant seekers apply for federal grants, loans and nonfinancial assistance, as well as private funding.

    Federal Business Opportunities

    Find helpful information about online procurement procedures and guidance for doing business or contracting with the federal government.

    Student Financial Aid

    Senator Carper is here to help students find the information they need to apply for student loans and achieve their educational goals.

    Service Academy Nominations

    Our country has the finest military academies in the world. Find out how you can be one of the students Senator Carper nominates to them.

    Internships

    An internship in Senator Carper's D.C. or Delaware offices is a great way for young Delawareans to learn more about the federal government and gain valuable experiences.

    Senate Youth Program

    Each year, distinguished Delaware high school students have the opportunity to spend a week in Washington, D.C. learning about government with students from across the country.

    Flag Requests

    Senator Carper and his office can arrange to have a flag flown above the U.S. Capitol building in honor of you or a loved one.

    Letter Requests

    Senator Carper is happy to provide a letter for you or a loved one to help commemorate a special occasion or event.

    Visit Washington, D.C.

    If you're planning to visit Washington, D.C., Senator Carper wants to help make your trip as memorable as possible. Click here to schedule tours through Senator Carper's office.

    Passport Assistance

    This page provides all the information and links a U.S. citizen would need to successfully apply for a passport.

    Senator Carper has always believed that speaking with and receiving feedback from Delawareans is the key to being an effective legislator. His goal as a U.S. Senator is to make sure that the people of Delaware are well represented in Washington, D.C. If you have any concerns, or would like to let Senator Carper know how you feel about a certain issue, you are welcome to reach out to the him through this website. 

    Senator Carper's e-newsletter is another great way to stay up to date with everything that is going on in Washington, D.C. and Delaware.

    In addition to maintaining his website and e-newsletter, Senator Carper has three offices in Delaware and one in Washington, D.C. to better serve the people of Delaware.
