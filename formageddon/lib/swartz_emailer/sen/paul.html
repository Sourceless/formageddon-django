Contact

 

It is an absolute honor to represent the people of Kentucky in the United States Senate.  Please use the following form to contact me with your comments and concerns regarding legislation.  Include your full name, address, phone and email to receive a written response.

If you are requesting help with a federal agency, please visit the "Help with Federal Agencies" portion of my website for timely assistance with your request.  Submitting your request for assistance on this comment page will cause a delay in processing, as your request must be forwarded to my State Office in Bowling Green. 

For MEDIA BOOKINGS/ PRESS INQUIRIES, please email press@paul.senate.gov  All press inquiries and media requests must be made in writing. Media requests submitted to this comment page will not be considered.

For scheduling requests, or to invite Sen. Paul to an event, please call my Washington, DC office directly at 202-224-4343. Scheduling requests and invitations submitted to this comment page will not be considered.
