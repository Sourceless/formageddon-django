    Mail Delay

    Mail delivered to the United States Capitol offices undergoes an extensive screening process for security reasons, thus causing a delay in delivery. Until the mail process is more timely, the best way to contact me or my Washington, DC staff is either by phone at (202) 224-2315 or via the contact form on this page.

    Assistance with a Federal Agency

    Senator Sherrod Brown's office is here to serve the people of Ohio. If you experience problems working with or getting answers from a federal government agency, Sherrod may be able to help. Click here to start a casework assistance request.

    Flag Requests

    U.S. Flags are available for purchase through Sherrod Brown's office. At your request and with sufficient notice, the flag can be flown over the U.S. Capitol building in honor of a person or event. Fill out the form.
