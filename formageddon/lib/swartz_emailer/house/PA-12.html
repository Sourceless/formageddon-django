Email Me - Thank You

Dear Neighbor:

Thank you for contacting me. Your thoughts, concerns, and questions are important to me and are the driving force behind my work in the 113th Congress.

I am committed to providing first-class customer service to you. We strive to provide a timely response to incoming correspondence, but there may be delays at times due to the large volume of mail our office receives.

If you need immediate assistance, I encourage you to call the Beaver office at (724) 359-1626, the Ross Township office at (412) 837-1361, the Johnstown office at (814) 619-3659, or the Washington office at (202) 225-2065.

It is an honor to serve and represent you. Thank you again for taking the time to share your thoughts.

 

Sincerely,
Keith Rothfus
Member of Congress
