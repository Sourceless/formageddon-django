Why do I have to fill out this form?

The U.S. House of Representatives has created a system that is designed to allow constituents to contact their Member of Congress, while reducing the amount of spam and junk mail that is sent to Congressional offices. By asking for a zip code, this system assures that the message is directed to the correct Member of Congress.
