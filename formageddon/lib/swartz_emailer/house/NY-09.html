Thank you for contacting me!

I appreciate hearing from you on the federal issues important to you.

To make correspondence with you more efficient and cost effective, I am now responding to your e-mails with an e-mail from me. Using this technology will help us stay in touch. However, please note that this email system is not designed to immediately address specific personal difficulties you may be experiencing with a federal agency. However, if your situation is an emergency or has a specific timeline, please contact my Brooklyn office at (718) 287-1142..

I strive to respond to all e-mails and letters within a few days after they are received. However, in some instances your response may be delayed either because I am experiencing an especially large volume of correspondence or because the issue or legislation about which you have communicated with me requires additional research. If your response is delayed, please know that I am working on it and, in any case, consider every constituent concern as it is received.
