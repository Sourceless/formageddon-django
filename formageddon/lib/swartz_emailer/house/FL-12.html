Why don't I have a regular email address?

The Write Your Representative system was designed to improve communications between constituents and their Representatives. Using the web form ensures you can easily find your Member of Congress and that your message is directed to the appropriate office. It also ensure your message gets to our office in a timely manner, so we can get a response back to you as quickly as possible.
